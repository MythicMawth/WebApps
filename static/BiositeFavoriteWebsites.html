Favorite websites are a weird concept. For the most part, I wouldn't actively open up my laptop to go on one of these webistes, but, when I'm on my laptop for fun, one or more of these websites are normally open.

Pokemon Showdown!

Pokemon Showdown! is an online Pokemon Battle simulator where you can battle Pokemon. That's it. I find it really fun and am now consistently ranked around 1700 in random battles. You can also build teams and do more serious battles here but even then I normally compete in the more "for fun" modes like Almost Any Ablility or Monotype.
